The extreme delicacy of my young lover won me to yield myself to the embarrassing ordeal – which proved less disconcerting than might have been expected; for at that time I had no idea a man possessed such a huge appendage for impregnating a Girl. His mere touch thrilled and electrified me, so that I responded to his gentle endearments – and presently we together achieved a most exquisite climax. 

And it seems incredible but I continued to recline upon the sand while he embraced me in the most affectionate way; and what seems even more disgraceful still, I was so enamoured with the delightful sense of sacred intimacy that we actually undressed and went surf bathing in a nude state then lay basking in the sunshine together and on returning to town he came to my flat and shared my bed for the night. 

He could not afford to marry then, and yet we could not pass– every day and every hour was one sweet dream of bliss; and now we are actually afraid to marry – lest we should break the sacred spell. 

My health noticably(sic) improved from the very outset and curiously my youthful appearance still remains so that I look more like merely twenty than nearly thirty – the agreeable sense of close companionship whether awake or sleeping has continued to be so refreshing and invigorating.
